Who is this magical application for and what does it do?

When the first house and class of Gryffindor was founded there was a student who was working on a secret project that would change the way wizards kept track of all their experimental spells and secret recipes. Before Nodebucket many of the wizards in the house were forced to write all their recipes and magic spells in a book which spell binding was prohibited. This was intentional as the professors needed access to see what students were working on and also it prevented the house of Professor Snape the Slytherins for created and evil spells which would put the rest of the houses in danger.


A group of wizards each made up of the different houses decided to create a secret society which would disregard this rule put forward by Hogwarts. Soon the secret society grew and the development of the NodeBucket started. Wizards were now able to organize all there potions in a TODO list and once they were completed they would be able to use their wands to move it into a part of the bucket known as the DONE list. Soon the application became so popular with the students that word quickly started to spread throughout Hogwarts of a secret society who created an application to break the house rules.

It was said that the student who started the project was locked away in a hidden part of Hogwarts until the application would be out versioned and usable by the local school browser which was not set to occur until 25 years after the application was used. Upon his release it is rumored that he already has an updated version stored in his memory which he will unleash upon his release.
